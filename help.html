Frequently Asked Questions

To add items to your inventory, navigate to the "Inventory" page and click the "Add Item" button. Fill in the required details, such as item name, category, quantity, and expiry date. You can also add an image and notes.

Go to the "Recipes" page. You can either search for recipes directly or use the "Suggest Recipes" feature to get recommendations based on your inventory and expiring items.
