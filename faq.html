Quick Categories

FoodPhoenix is a comprehensive food waste reduction platform that helps individuals and communities track their food inventory, share surplus food, and reduce waste through smart management tools. Our platform provides:

  • Digital inventory tracking with expiry date monitoring
  • Food sharing capabilities to donate surplus items
  • Recipe suggestions based on available ingredients
  • Waste analytics and reporting
  • Community features to connect with local food banks

Yes! FoodPhoenix is completely free to use. We believe that reducing food waste should be accessible to everyone. Our platform is supported by community donations and partnerships with local organizations committed to sustainability.

Adding items to your inventory is simple:

  1. Navigate to the "Add Item" page from your inventory
  2. Fill in the required information: name, category, quantity, and expiry date
  3. Optionally add notes and upload an image
  4. Click "Add Item" to save

Your item will be automatically added to your inventory with status indicators (Fresh, Urgent, or Expired) based on the expiry date.

Our expiry date tracking system automatically categorizes your items:

  • Fresh (Green): Items with more than 3 days until expiry
  • Urgent (Yellow): Items expiring within 1-3 days
  • Expired (Red): Items that have passed their expiry date

You can set up notifications to receive alerts when items are approaching their expiry date.

Yes! You can manage your inventory items:

  • Edit: Click the pencil icon next to any item to modify its details
  • Delete: Click the trash icon to remove items from your inventory
  • Share: Use the share icon to offer items to the community

All changes are saved automatically to your local storage.

Food sharing allows you to offer surplus food items to your community:

  1. Select items from your inventory that you want to share
  2. Set pickup location and time preferences
  3. Add any special instructions or dietary information
  4. Post your offer to the community board
  5. Connect with interested community members

This helps reduce waste while supporting your local community.

We prioritize safety in food sharing:

  • Only share items that are within their expiry date
  • Meet in public, well-lit locations
  • Communicate clearly about item conditions
  • Use our community rating system to build trust
  • Report any concerns to our support team

Always use your best judgment and follow local food safety guidelines.

Here are effective strategies to reduce food waste:

  • Plan meals: Create weekly meal plans and shopping lists
  • Store properly: Learn optimal storage conditions for different foods
  • Use leftovers: Transform leftovers into new meals
  • Freeze surplus: Freeze items before they expire
  • Compost: Compost unavoidable food waste
  • Share surplus: Use our platform to share with others

When food expires, consider these options:

  • Check if it's still safe: Some foods are safe beyond their "best before" date
  • Compost: Most expired food can be composted
  • Animal feed: Some items can be fed to pets or livestock
  • Dispose properly: Use appropriate waste disposal methods

Remember: "Best before" dates indicate quality, not safety. Use your senses to determine if food is still good.

Your privacy and data security are important to us:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage
  • We don't collect or store personal information on our servers
  • Your inventory data remains private and secure
  • You can export your data anytime
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all communications

For additional security, consider regularly backing up your data.

  • Safari (version 12+)
  • Edge (version 79+)
  • Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Mobile)

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser.
